Eren Jeager

The man who started the rumbling

Here's a timeline of Eren Jeagers' life:

  • Year 845 - Watched his mom get eaten by a titan due to the fall of Shinganshina (his hometown)
  • His father Grisha Jeager injects him with titan serum which transformed into a titan eating his father and becoming the Attack Titan as well as the Founding Titan
  • Year 847 - Enlisted in the 104th Cadet Corp in order to take the fight for humanity inside the walls along with Armin and Mikasa
  • Year 850 - Transforms into into a titan after saving Armins' life and helps the Cadets survive the titan onslaught brought on by the Colossal titan in Trost District
  • Fills the hole made by the Colossal by plugging it with a giant boulder
  • Becomes a member of the Scout Regiment along with his comrades and goes on the 57th Scout Expedition in which the Female Titan attack and ambushes him and Levis' squad. Eren fights her off unsuccessfully
  • The Female is revealed to be Annie Leonhart a cadet in the 104th Cadet Corp, Eren fights her off in Stohess District within Wall Sina
  • A day after the Stohess battle Reiner and Bertholdt reveal that they're the Colossal and Armoured Titan, and get into a battle with Eren and the Scouts, the fight ends with a loss and Eren gets captured
  • Reiner leaves the giant forest in Wall Maria with Eren while the Scouts go after them, once Eren gets away from Reiner, the titan that ate his mom as a child appears and Hannes gets eaten by it, Eren temporarily controls the titans around due to touching a titan with royal blood and the Scouts escape
  • Mid Year 850 - Eren gets captured by Historia's father in order to return the Founding Titan back to the royal family, Eren also finds out the truth of what his father did to obtain it
  • The Scouts reclaim Wall Maria and learn the truth of their world which was in Eren's basement
  • Late Year 850 - The Scouts continue their expeditions outside the walls and arrive at the sea
  • Year 851-854 - Eren and Scouts learn more and more about the outside world and invade Marley
  • Year 854 - Attacks the Marleyean intenment zone killing countless civilians as well as killing and eat the Warhammer Titan
  • Gets jailed for acting out and escapes to meet Zeke and begin the rumbling
  • Gets in contact with Zeke before dying after getting his head blown off by Gabi
  • Activates the rumbling after it was revealed that he influenced his father to take the Founding Titan from the Reiss family using the powers of the Attack Titan
  • Telepathically informs all Eldians that the rumbling will wipe out all of humanity beyond the walls
  • Killed 80% of all humanity beyond the walls
